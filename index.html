About Us

Readly is the largest platform where you can find an unending collection of quality books, newsletters and articles at a reasonable price. If you are a book-worm, then we welcome you to explore out our collection and find pleasure.

Readly is a social initiative to instill the habit of book reading in youth and old. Our huge collection of printed resources will even help you put your eyes off-screen while at the same time boosting your knowledge and keep you occupied in your leisures. The Readly website has been recognized as one of the best social development initiative by the Ministry of Education on 18th June, 2018.
